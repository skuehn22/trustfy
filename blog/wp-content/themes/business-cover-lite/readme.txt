== Description ==
Business Cover Lite multipurpose WordPress has used for most of all type of business. Its used for the multipurpose business and all other business.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to Customize Theme =

Just click on Appearance -> customize
Then you will find section to edit and update it

= How to Customize Header Phone and email =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Business Cover Header Phone and email

= How to Customize Header Social =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Business Cover Social Link

= How to Customize Header Home banner =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Header Image

= How to Customize footer Copyright =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Business Cover Lite Footer

= How to Customize footer Widget =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Widget
